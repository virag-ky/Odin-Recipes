Fresh Homemade Salsa

If you love chips then this recipe is perfect for you. This is a very simple and easy recipe. And not just for appetizers but for a snack is great also, you can quickly prep the ingredients and chill it in the fridge for about an hour and it's ready to eat. It makes about 12 servings in cups. So if you are looking for a fresh and crispy appetizer then this is an excellent choice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons canned green chilies
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Combine tomatoes, red onion, yellow onion, green chilies, lime juice, cilantro, garlic, cumin, and salt in a food processor.
  2. Pulse processor until mixture is combined, yet remains chunky.
  3. Transfer to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
